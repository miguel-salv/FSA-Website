About Us

Our Mission

The Filipino Student Association (FSA) at Carnegie Mellon University aims to promote Filipino culture, traditions, and values within the CMU community. We provide a supportive environment for Filipino and Filipino-American students while sharing our rich heritage with the broader campus.

What We Do

FSA hosts cultural celebrations, educational workshops, community service initiatives, and social events throughout the academic year. We collaborate with other cultural organizations at CMU and in the Pittsburgh area to foster inclusion and diversity.

Barkada Dance Team

Our Barkada dance team is a vibrant part of FSA that showcases traditional Filipino dances, including the iconic tinikling. The team performs at various campus events and participates in the Dancers Symposium every semester, sharing the beauty and grace of Filipino cultural dance with the CMU community.

Join Our Community

Whether you're Filipino, Filipino-American, or simply interested in learning about Filipino culture, we welcome you to join our community! No prior knowledge of Filipino culture or language is required - just bring your enthusiasm and curiosity.

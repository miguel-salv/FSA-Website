Our Alumni Legacy

Our alumni network consists of former FSA members who continue to support and engage with our community after graduation. These individuals have made significant contributions to FSA during their time at Carnegie Mellon and now serve as mentors, advisors, and professional connections for current members.

Staying Connected

We maintain strong ties with our alumni through reunions, networking events, and collaborative initiatives. Their ongoing involvement enriches our organization and provides valuable perspectives from those who have transitioned from campus life to professional careers.
